ABOUT US

Redemption is a non-governmental organization founded in 1895 with a view to prevent the deployment of Weapons of Mass Destruction(MDWs) worldwide under any circumstances. The organization was established in response to the rapid development of advanced military technology since the industrial revolution, which may lead to the introduction of attack methods capable of wiping out human lives on a massive scale.
